Product Development

Product Development encompasses the entire journey from idea to launch, ensuring the product is functional, user-friendly, and meets market demands.

Request a Demo

Product Development is a challenging and complex process, best done internally. Recognizing this need Quad One works with clients as an extension of their development teams, providing best-suited solutions to complex problems. We also provide turnkey services for product development.

We provide product development services using a wide range of technologies including .NET and PHP.

We follow a well-established and documented development methodology including detailed requirements analysis, project planning, design walk-throughs, development, testing, deployment and client handover with training. We support the product post-implementation depending on client requirements.

In Product Development, our emphasis is on

  • Understanding client requirements completely
  • Architecture the solution in such a way that its flexible and extensible
  • Focusing on Front end design and development so that user experience is maximized
  • Being flexible enough to incorporate mid-development changes
  • Building a front end walk-through for the client so that subsequent changes are minimized
  • Implementing using the best suited technology and not just the ‘latest’ technologies
  • A rigorous QA process
  • Handholding the client till he is confident enough to manage on his own
  • Detailed documentation
  • Communication with the client on a regular basis (daily, bi-weekly)

We have built a large number of applications in the Pharmaceutical, Healthcare, Education, Banking, Government and Internet space and have an experienced set of Business Analysts and Developers who understand custom application development peculiarities.

We understand the business needs of our clients and undertake turnkey projects or provide experienced resources on a dedicated basis.
